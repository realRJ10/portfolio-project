Calvin Klein

Euphoria

Euphoria by Calvin Klein is a Amber Floral fragrance for women. It was launched in 2005. Euphoria was created by Dominique Ropion, Carlos Benaim and Loc Dong. Top notes are Pomegranate, Persimmon and Green Accord; middle notes are Black Orchid, Lotus and Champaca; base notes are Mahogany, Amber, Black Violet and Whipped Cream. This perfume is the winner of award FiFi Award Fragrance Of The Year Women`s Luxe 2006. One of the world's leading lifestyle brands, Calvin Klein is synonymous with authentic modern minimalist style. Everything it creates —clothing, accessories, home design—becomes a timeless classic. The label's award-winning fragrances are no exception. Calvin Klein's portfolio of bold and iconic scents continues to redefine the world of perfume. Free the fantasy: embark on a journey without limits, sparked by a new found freedom of modern sensuality. Provocative and sensual, euphoria for women is inspired by the orchid’s mythical, sexy and exotic notes, creating a truly mysterious and feminine perfume. Intensely luxurious, euphoria eau de parfum lasts for hours and garners compliments day and night. The top opens with a burst of pomegranate and succulent persimmon followed by a mysterious and intoxicating blend of rich orchid and dewy lotus blossom. The drydown leaves a lasting signature of liquid amber and mahogany wood paired with sensual musks and an addictive cream accord. Evocative of an orchid about to bloom and the curves of a woman’s body, the polished bottle is a modern interpretation of sensuality.
